RELATIVE


PRONOUNCE

WHO.
Digunakan untuk menggantikan subjek manusia dalam klausa relatif.

1.The girl who won the competition is my sister.

2.I have a friend who speaks multiple languages.

3.The person who called you left a message.

WHOSE.
Menunjukkan kepemilikan, dapat digunakan untuk orang atau benda.

1. The man whose car was stolen reported it to the police.

2. The student whose project impressed everyone received an award.

3. She has a friend whose house is near the beach.

WHICH.
Menggantikan subjek atau objek benda dalam klausa relatif.

1. I found the book which you were looking for.

2. The car which he bought last week is already in the garage.

3. The movie, which was released last month, received positive

THAT.
Bisa menggantikan subjek atau objek, baik manusia maupun benda, dalam klausa relatif.

1. The cat that is sitting on the windowsill is mine.

2. The shirt that she bought is on sale.

3. I need a pen that writes smoothly.

WHOM.
Menggantikan objek manusia dalam klausa relatif.

1. The professor whom I admire is giving a lecture.

2. The artist whom they interviewed is quite famous.

3. He has a cousin whom we met at the party.

WHERE.
Digunakan untuk menggantikan tempat dalam klausa relatif.

1. This is the park where we used to play as children.

2. The restaurant where we had dinner last night was fantastic.

3. I found the street where he lives.

WHEN.
Menggantikan waktu dalam klausa relatif..

1. The day when we first met is etched in my memory.

2. Do you remember the time when we went on that road trip?

3. I miss the period when we were all together in college.

KESIMPULAN.
Relative pronoun seperti who, whose, whom, which, that, where, dan when memainkan peran penting dalam memperkaya kalimat-kalimat bahasa Inggris. Den gan penggunaan yang tepat, kita dapat mengungkapkan informasi dengan lebih terperinci dan m enyenangkan.
